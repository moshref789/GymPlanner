At the top of the page, there is a navigation bar that includes options such as Home, My Programs, Trainer, Video, Search Video, Privacy, About Us, User Account, and Theme, along with Login and Register buttons on the right side.
In the center of the page, there is a welcome message that introduces the platform and explains that users can manage workout programs, read privacy information, access trainer pages, and watch exercise videos.
Below the welcome message, there are two main sections:


Next Step:
This section guides users to visit the My Programs page to view their workout tables and program details.


Exercise Videos:
This section contains a dropdown menu that allows users to choose an exercise category (such as "Back") to view related workout videos.


At the bottom of the section, there are two human body illustrations showing muscle groups. One of the illustrations highlights specific upper-body muscles, particularly in the chest and shoulder areas, in red to indicate targeted workout muscles.
